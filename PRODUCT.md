# Product

**Register:** product

## Product purpose

Pitch Recognizer is an ear-training and tuning web app. It listens through the
microphone and detects the pitch the user sings or plays. It has two modes:

- **Trainer**: a guessing game. A note is shown on the staff; the user matches it
  by ear, building score and streak.
- **Tuner**: a continuous chromatic tuner showing the nearest note, frequency, and
  cents deviation.

## Users

Music learners and instrumentalists who want to train relative pitch or tune up.
Comfortable with note names; not necessarily with music theory jargon.

## Tone

Quiet, precise, confidence-building. The interface stays out of the way so the
sound is the focus. Feedback is immediate and unambiguous (in tune / not yet).

## Strategic principles

- The readout is the hero. Everything else is chrome.
- Never punish exploration. Skipping and wrong notes cost a streak, never block.
- Numbers (notes, frequencies, cents, scores) are data and read as data: monospace.

## Anti-references

Gamified, badge-heavy practice apps. Skeuomorphic analog tuner needles and
brushed-metal textures.
